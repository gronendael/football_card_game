# Progression and monetization (design)

## Franchise progression

W/L, fan support, playbook/deck unlocks, coordinator hires, branding, stadium (cosmetic). League structure (seasons, divisions) — light in V1; promotion/relegation optional post-V1 ([Ideas.md](Ideas.md)).

## Player progression (training-first)

- **Training** is primary long-term growth ([Gameplay_Summary.md](Gameplay_Summary.md): training-based evolution, not automatic per-game stat inflation).
- Species grants **efficiency multipliers** on XP toward specific stats (small; see [Balancing.md](Balancing.md)).
- Traits may unlock at milestones (species-weighted pools).

## Recruitment

- **Prospects** generated per region/season—not a shared global card pool.
- Scout reports surface archetype, species, and outlier flags.
- Draft tiers control roll quality variance, not guaranteed 10s.

## Monetization philosophy

**Toward:** recruitment slots, prospect discovery, species template packs (new *pool access*, not stat supremacy), regional scouting, cosmetics, coordinators, training facilities, team/stadium branding.

**Away from:** shared hero collection, buy-power-stat, duplicate chase for the same global `player_id`.

**Avoid pay-to-win:** no purchasable permanent +1 stats; convenience and cosmetics OK.

## Roster limits (unchanged)

Roster 50, lineup 17–22, deck 8–16, playbook 3–12.

## Scouting regions (V1 flavor)

Named regions (e.g. core worlds, frontier belt, nebula academies) weight species and archetype pools in recruitment—not deep lore.
