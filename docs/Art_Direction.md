# Art direction

## Priority order

1. **Football readability** — ball, LOS, downs, zones, play outcome
2. **Position and archetype** — shape, label, role icon
3. **Species silhouette** — distinguishable at mobile chip size (~26px field markers)
4. Sci-fi flavor — palettes, UI frames, stadium backgrounds

## Species visuals

- Distinct **silhouette** (height/mass/proportions); limit limb complexity on field tokens
- 2–3 accent colors; avoid noisy patterns on chips
- Same archetype across species should still read as that archetype

## UI

Sports broadcast / tactical HUD; alien typography and frames OK; **no** spell VFX on the field that obscure ball or LOS.

## Field markers

- **Field lineup markers**: player display names on formation tiles ([Screens.md](Screens.md))
- **Tick sim playback**: separate moving chips (Debug); keep motion readable, not cluttered

## Anti-patterns

Superhero poses, magic particles, parody mascots, clutter that hides the tile grid.
