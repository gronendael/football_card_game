# Worldbuilding — Intergalactic Football League (IFL)

## Tone

Professional sports broadcast energy: competitive, readable, grounded. **Not** superhero combat, fantasy magic, or parody sports.

## The league

The **Intergalactic Football League (IFL)** is a multi-world professional football competition. Franchises represent **homeworlds** (or major orbital stations). Rules, downs, zones, and play structure remain football-first; sci-fi flavor explains *who* competes and *why* rosters differ.

## V1 scope (keep light)

- League name, season structure (divisions, W/L; playoffs depth TBD)
- One-line **football culture** per franchise homeworld
- **Species-based cultures** as tendencies (pace, physicality, discipline)—not supernatural abilities
- 2–3 **rivalry** pairs (flavor hooks only)
- **Scouting regions** (names + recruit pool weights; see [Progression.md](Progression.md))

## Franchises

Each team: homeworld, colors, stadium, fan identity. Rosters are **unique generated players** bound to that franchise—not globally shared collectible cards every user must own.

## Species in culture

Species describe how worlds develop athletes (training pipelines, body types, tactical traditions). They influence **development probability** and **style**, not match abilities outside football stats and resolver math.

## Out of scope for V1 lore

Galactic wars, magic systems, deep religion/politics codexes, multi-season narrative arcs.
